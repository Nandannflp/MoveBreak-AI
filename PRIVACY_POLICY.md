# Privacy Policy - MoveBreak AI

**Last Updated: March 14, 2026**

Your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how MoveBreak AI handles your data.

## 1. Data Storage & Sync
MoveBreak AI uses a hybrid storage approach:
- **Local Storage**: Your activity data is cached locally for offline performance.
- **Secure Cloud Sync**: To ensure your data isn't lost when you change devices, we sync your stats to a secure, private backend managed by the app using Firebase and Google Cloud.

## 2. Secure Authentication
- **Methods**: We offer Email/Password and Google Sign-In.
- **Passwords**: We never store plain-text passwords. All credentials are hashed and managed by Firebase's secure industry-standard infrastructure.
- **OAuth**: Google login uses secure official tokens, and we never see or store your Google password.

## 3. Data Ownership & Security
- **Strict Isolation**: Only you can access your data. We use server-side security rules to prevent any other user or third party from viewing your records.
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do NOT sell or share your data with advertisers, analytics companies, or external data centers.
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and at rest within the database.

## 4. Permissions
- **Camera**: Used locally for posture analysis. No images are uploaded to the cloud.
- **Activity**: Syncs with the secure database to maintain your wellness streaks.

## 5. Account Deletion
You have the right to be forgotten. Deleting your account from the app settings permanently wipes all associated data from both our cloud database and your device.

## 5. Contact Us
For any questions regarding your privacy, please contact: `support@movebreakai.com`
